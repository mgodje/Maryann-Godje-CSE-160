Setting the Scene:

Here is a world, a starry night in a grassy field. There is an owl, sitting on a tree branch in the middle.

The owl is in the middle of a forest green sphere and a light purple cube. Finally, there is a yellow cube that is the source of the lighting in this scene.

There are buttons to turn on and off Normal and the lighting. Use these buttons to turn the normal and lighting on and off.

There are also 4 sliders: Camera Angle, Light X, Light Y, and Light Z.

The Camera Angle allows you to swivel around 360 degrees. The Light X, Y, and Z sliders allow you to move the light source around the scene in the x, y, and z directions.

Feel free to use the AWSDQE buttons to move around and rotate. You can also use the arror keys to move around.

Enjoy the scene!
